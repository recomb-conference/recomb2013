Travel Fellowships

ISCB travel fellowship
Thanks to generous support from the International Society for Computational Biology (ISCB), we have roughly 5,000 USD available to fund about 10 attendees. These awards are only available to ISCB student members. To apply, please

  1. Download and fill out this ISCB travel fellowship application form.
  2. Send the filled and signed form as a single pdf file to ruijiang@tsinghua.edu.cn, with "RECOMB 2013 --- ISCB Travel Fellowship Application" as the subject of your email.
ISCB wishes to thank Akamai Technologies for their support of ISCB student travel fellowships to RECOMB 2013.

NSF travel fellowship
Thanks to generous support from the US National Science Foundation (NSF), we have roughly 12,000 USD available to fund up to 24 attendees. These awards are only available to students in US institutions. To apply, please

  1. Download and fill out this NSF travel fellowship application form.
  2. Send the filled and signed form as a single pdf file to ruijiang@tsinghua.edu.cn, with "RECOMB 2013 --- NSF Travel Fellowship Application" as the subject of your email.

Tsinghua/TNLIST travel fellowship
Thanks to generous support from Tsinghua University and the Tsinghua National Laboratory for Information Science and Technology (TNLIST), we have roughly 5,000 USD available to fund about 10 attendees. These awards are available to all attendees who are not eligible for the above two fellowships. To apply, please

  1. Download and fill out this TNLIST travel fellowship application form.
  2. Send the filled and signed form as a single pdf file to ruijiang@tsinghua.edu.cn, with "RECOMB 2013 --- TNLIST Travel Fellowship Application" as the subject of your email.

All applicants should have completed the online registration at http://online.recomb2013.org before the application. Due to the limited amount of travel fellowships, each person can apply for only one travel fellowship. Applications should be submitted no later than March 10, 2013. You should receive an email ackanowedging the receipt of your application within 3 workdays. If not, please resubmit. The result will be announced to each applicant by email around March 15, 2013.
