Welcome to RECOMB 2013！

Welcome to RECOMB 2013, the 17th Annual International Conference on Research in Computational Molecular Biology!

RECOMB 2013 will take place in beautiful Beijing from April 7-10, 2013. The conference aims at bridging the computational, mathematical, statistical, and biological sciences. The conference features keynote talks by preeminent scientists in life sciences, proceeding presentations of peer-reviewed research papers in computational biology, and poster sessions on the latest research progress. As usual, authors of accepted papers will be invited to submit an edited version of the manuscript to the Journal of Computational Biology for the publication of a special issue.

RECOMB 2013 will be combined with a satellite workshop called RECOMB-seq 2013, dated April, 11-12, 2013. Please visit http://bioinfo.au.tsinghua.edu.cn/recomb2013/satellite.html for detailed information.
