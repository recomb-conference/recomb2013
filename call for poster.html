Call for Posters

RECOMB 2013 invites the submission of abstracts for the poster track.

Please visit the "Submission" section of this website to submit a one-page abstract (about 200 words, plain justified text in 10pt character size, formatted to fit A4: 21 x 29.7 cm) of your poster, including title, author(s), affiliation, e-mail, and abstract text. Accepted poster abstracts will be published in the conference book of abstracts.
