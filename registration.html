PAYMENT & CANCELLATION 

Full payment should accompany registration forms to complete the registration. All price quotes are in Chinese RMB Yuan (CNY). Click to check latest Exchanging Ratios provided by Bank of China.

For payment in a foreign currency, we accept the following three methods:

Payment Methods

Option 1: Payment by Credit Card (preferred) *
Acceptable credit cards are MasterCard, Visa and American Express. Please complete your credit card details on the registration form. The payment will be billed in CNY, and the actual amount charged to your account in your currency is decided by your credit card company.

Option 2: Payment by cheque 
Please make cheques /bank drafts payable to: CICCST/RECOMB2013. Please print the delegate name and affiliation on the back of the check or bank drafts.
